Who is Cornell?

Hi, I am Cornell Coulon a Web Developer who loves working on the Front-End & I reside in the Greater San Antonio Area. I spent the first part of my career as a Chef in the foodservice industry. I was a protege of Emeril Lagasse & I owned a catering business in the Washington, DC metro area. It was in that industry that I learned the value of servant leadership. I chose to hang up my touque, and pursue my desire to become a developer, starting with self directed learning, and later attending a web development bootcamp in Bellevue, WA to accelerate my learning. Currently I am a Web Developer at Trinity University, and I am completing my B.S. in Computer Science.
